A DangerZone Faithfuls Message.

For a comparable half of my life I have been on an incredible journey. I could have never guessed back in the early 2000s that I would get to go down such a crazy path due to the rather limited musical abilities of just a guy from Madziva. Up up Mash Central . It was a long time coming. It took time and effort , practice. Nhubu! , Digital! tell them I did not earn this title " Dancehall Doctor" overnight. 

I could have not been this far if it wasnt for the support I got from all of you. Well firstly from my family . My brothers and sisters who have stood up for me every way everyday. Its tough growing up without parents . Miss them everyday. 

 

HKD Fans. You are my family. I could never thank you all enough for the love and support you’ve shown me over these years until to date. DZ!! Everywhere me go there is love in the sky, Zim my Home vakungoti HKD!! , UK HKD!! , Canada HKD!! , Australia , China , PaJozi. Ingori HKD!!. DangerZone For Life.. It’s means so much to me. THANK YOU!!! And now...We rise ..And rise again. 

Now with a live Band.

First of all, wow, what an experience.  To say that God moves in mysterious ways would be a complete understatement. I'm still not completely sure how this all happened this quickly, it seems like yesterday when I decided to pursue this and finally(in the Rock's Voice.) we have a band, but not just any band, a band that will influence millions of people with great songs, a band that will have a legacy and somehow also find longevity.  Freeman is now an established brand and to merely be part of something that has shaped and changed the music industry I am grateful. There are many unsung heroes who have made Dangerzone what it is today.   

To long-time fans of Freeman I understand what this HKD band means to you. To the new ones , Welcome and enjoy the ride.  I hope that together we can begin a journey to get to know each other  ultimately with the understanding that we the band are following the heart Dancehall in the best way we know how.  It is our desire to go into the studio and create music that fans dont only dance to but get inspired with in every aspect of life.

And finally, I hope you realize that you are loved by our Creator, not for what you do, but because you were created by Him. I hope that through our music realise purpose soothe when you are low and set the mood when you are groovy Our music has only one misssion .For you to be happy . Spread love and spread love.  After all that is what this music is about.   

Thanks,   

Freeman aka Sly
