A DangerZone Faithfuls Message.

For the years I have served in the HKD kraal/family, with a heavy heart, I would want to say, If it were not for the family, fans and friends behind HKD, all my efforts would have been in vain hence i would not be where i am today. Legalism says, God will love us if we change, but the gospel says, God will change us because he loves us. I confess. Personally, I've been changed through the grace of God. My scars changed to stars. I travailed until I prevailed. 

Now we get played on Trace Africa , Many thanks across the globe to the broadcasting stations. Special mentions Zim and UK radio stations. It is never easy to be in a family where parents are six feet under. I salute my brothers, my sisters and my relatives for having stood by me even when the worst seemed worse. It has taught me that behind storms, there is rainbow. It is my dream to sow love where there is hatred; to sow faith where there is doubt; to sow hope where there is despair and to sow light where there is darkness. Besides being a singer, I have a heart for my community (Dangerzone)

Now with a live Band.

I can only say wow, what an experience.  To say that God moves in mysterious ways would be a complete understatement. I'm still not completely sure how this all happened for me, it seems like yesterday when I decided to pursue this and finally(in the Rock's Voice.) we have a band, but not just any band, Dangerzone Band. a band that will have a legacy and also thrive to find longevity.   Freeman as an artist is now an established brand and to merely be part of something that has shaped and changed the music industry I am grateful. There are many unsung heroes who have made Dangerzone what it is today.   

To long-time fans of Freeman I understand what this HKD band means to you. To the new ones , Welcome and enjoy the ride.  I hope that together we can begin a journey to get to know each other.   We the band are following the heart Dancehall in the best way we know how.  It is our desire to go into the studio and create music that fans dont only dance to but get inspired with in every aspect of life.

And finally, I hope you realize that you are loved by our Creator, not for what you do, but because you were created by Him. I hope that through our music you realise purpose , get soothed when you are low and set you up when you are groovy.    Our music has only one misssion .For you to be happy . Spread love and spread love.  After all that is what this music is about.   

Thank you for the support.   

Freeman aka Sly
